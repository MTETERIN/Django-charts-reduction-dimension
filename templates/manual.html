Welcome to the data mining system.

This system was created in cooperation with Mondi Group and Saint-Petersburg State Institute of Technology to perform analysis of big industrial data.

Right now the system has 3 main analytics modules:

  • - timelineTech params
  • - graphic_eqData graphs
  • - trending_upData Mining methods

All you need, is to choose parameter(s) and defect type.

After that just press plot button and the system will give you graphs.

The system is made with Django Framework and Materialize framework and using SQLiteto manage data.

You can contact with administrator by choosing the feedback"Feedback" button in the side menu.
